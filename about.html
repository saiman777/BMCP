About Us

BMCP believes in Smart technology & efficient process management are the pillars of delivering great customer experience. The platform has been adopted across many categories such as smart devices, home appliances, automobile & other consumer durables such as furniture. Started in 2016, Delhi-based BMCP is engaged in transforming the after-sales service experience. BMCP currently works with a number of top device brands, retailers, distributors, insurers, service providers and carriers such as Amazon, AmTrust, Apple, Croma, Godrej, Huawei, Ingram Micro, Micromax, Motorola/Lenovo, Nokia, OnePlus, OPPO, Panasonic, RealMe, Redington, Reliance Jio, Reliance Retail, Sangeetha Mobiles, Vodafone, and Xiaomi, among others.
